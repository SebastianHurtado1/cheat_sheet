<center>

# Ace Jump / Search

</center>

### Jump to selection
> `control + ;`

<br>

### Jump and select word
> `control + option + ;`

<br>

### Jump to first character of line
> `control + shiftf + ;`

<br>


### Jump to first character of line or end of line
> `control + ; + up or left or up or down`

<br>

### Jump to line
> `command + l`

<br>
